What Is Arm Liposuction?

Arm liposuction is a surgical procedure that removes excess subcutaneous fat from the upper arms using small, hidden incisions. It targets areas that commonly create the “bat wing” appearance or cause arms to look bulky in fitted clothing.

The goal is not simply to “make the arms smaller,” but to create a smoother, more balanced contour and improved definition that looks natural.

Who Is a Good Candidate?

Ideal Candidate

  • Stable body weight and realistic expectations.
  • Localised fat pockets in upper arms/triceps.
  • Good skin elasticity (vital for tightening).
  • Non-smoker or willing to stop temporarily.

Not Suitable If

  • Significant loose hanging skin is the main issue.
  • Severe obesity or fluctuating weight.
  • Poor skin elasticity (skin may not retract).
